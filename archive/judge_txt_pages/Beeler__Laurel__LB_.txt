SETTLEMENT CONFERENCES:
Through at least May 1, 2020, the court (under the new General Orders 72 and 73, available at
https://cand.uscourts.gov/notices/information-regarding-covid-19/
) will not have any in-person settlement conferences. The court will evaluate with the parties whether in-person settlement conferences will be postponed or go forward through videoconferencing .
The court does not allow audio-only participation. Within three business days of receiving notice of these procedures, counsel must confer and file a joint statement with their views about whether the settlement conference should be postponed or go forward by videoconferencing.
