SCHEDULING NOTES REGARDING COVID-19 PUBLIC HEALTH EMERGENCY.
These notes apply to all matters scheduled before Judge Westmore, until further notice.
Civil Cases:
1. Jury trials and bench trials may proceed in accordance with the logistical considerations necessitated by the Court’s safety protocols.
2. Until further notice, each judge will determine whether to hold a hearing or decide a civil matter on the papers.
If Judge Westmore believes a hearing is necessary, the hearing will be held by Zoom videoconference and a notice will be filed on the docket.
This applies to motion hearings, case management conferences, pretrial conferences, Alternative Dispute Resolution ("ADR") proceedings, and bench trials. For specific instructions on telephone or video appearances, see the docket on PACER, the assigned judge’s schedule of upcoming proceedings (located on each judge’s web page at
www.cand.uscourts.gov
/judges
), or the
ADR webpage
.
