Civil Case Management
Calendar

Criminal
Calendar
:
Out-of-custody status conferences and supervised release violations are conducted via Zoom on Friday at 8:30 a.m.
In-custody status conferences and supervised release violations will be held on Friday at 10:00 a.m.
Sentencing, change of plea, motions, and
any evidentiary hearings will be heard in person on  Friday at 10:00
a.m.
Re-notices:
Re-noticed motions filed in cases reassigned to Judge Thompson
are to be re-noticed for a hearing date of or after November 15, 2022. 
Re-noticed motions may be reset as the Court’s calendar requires.
New Motions:
New motions filed are
to be noticed for a hearing for or after November 1, 2022. Please keep in mind
the dates of unavailability when setting for hearing.
Case Management Conferences re Reassignment Order:
If parties file their
Joint Case Management Statement prior to October 14, 2022, the Court may be
able to schedule their Case Management Conference during a special set of
conferences that will be held on in the mornings during the last two weeks of
October, subject to availability.
