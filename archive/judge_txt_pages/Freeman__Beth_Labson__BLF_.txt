PARTIES SHALL SUBMIT
CHAMBERS COPIES OF ALL PAPERS WHEN FILED. ***Chambers Copies shall be delivered
to the Clerk's Office by noon following the day of filing.***
Courtroom attire is
expected for all attorneys appearing at hearings that are conducted by Zoom
Webinar.
CRIMINAL MATTERS
All Criminal Matters
will be handled in person, in the courtroom.
Members of the public,
family, and friends are welcome to attend court hearings conducted in the
courtroom.
CIVIL MATTERS
Judge Freeman will
return to full in-person hearings for all civil law and motion hearings beginning
July 1, 2024, unless otherwise ordered by the court.
Civil case management
conferences will continue to be held by Zoom Webinar until further notice.
Final pretrial conferences,
jury instruction conferences, and all evidentiary hearings will be conducted in
person, in the courtroom.
Jury selection and trial
will be conducted in person, in the courtroom. Witnesses are expected to appear
in person for testimony unless otherwise approved by the Court.
The Court is NOT able to
accommodate some attorneys appearing in person and others by Zoom.
These arrangements may
be modified if public health conditions require a return to more limited
in-person contact.
