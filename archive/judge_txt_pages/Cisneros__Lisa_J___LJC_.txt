TO
ALL civil and criminal litigants with matters currently pending before
United States District Magistrate Judge Lisa J. Cisneros., Northern District of
California.  The Court issues the following additional
guidance:
1.
All
Civil Case Management Conferences will be held and recorded via zoom.
2.
All Civil Law and Motion hearings will be held
in person. To the extent the Court finds that a motion may be decided on the
papers and a hearing is not necessary, the Court will alert the parties
.
COURT
SCHEDULE:
1.
Criminal
Law and Motion hearings will be held in person on Tuesday’s at 1:30 p.m. on
non-Criminal Duty months. The Court’s Criminal Duty Schedule is available at
https://www.cand.uscourts.gov/calendars/duty-schedules/
.
2.
Civil
Law and Motion hearings will held on Tuesdays 10:30 a.m. in person during non-Criminal
Duty months; during Criminal Duty Months (see dates below), hearings will be held Thursdays at
1:30 p.m. in person.
3.
Civil
Case Management Conferences are heard Thursdays at 1:30 p.m. via Zoom
4.   Pretrial Conferences are heard Fridays at 2:00 p.m. Trials will start on Mondays from 8:30 a.m.- 1:30 p.m.
4.
Settlement
conferences are held on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, starting at 10:00 a.m- Settlement conferences are generally held in person.  AT&T Telephonic
pre-settlement conference meetings are held Thursdays at 10:30 a.m.
5.   Judge Cisneros will handle Criminal Duty during the following months: December 22, 2024-December 29, 2024, April 2025, October 2025.
UNAVAILABLE
DATES:

take notice of Magistrate Judge Lisa J. Cisneros will not be available on the
below dates. The Court will not be in session during these periods of
unavailability.
If
urgency requires, counsel in any case assigned or referred to Magistrate Judge Cisneros
may request a hearing before the General Duty Judge pursuant to Civil Local
Rule 7-1(c).
