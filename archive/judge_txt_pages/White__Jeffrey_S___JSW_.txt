on

CIVIL LAW AND MOTION:
Pending further order of the Court, all civil law and motion matters will be submitted on the papers. See General Orders 72, 73.
If the Court determines a hearing is required, the hearing will be conducted by ZOOM during the Court’s normal law and motion calendar at 9:00 am, unless otherwise specified by the Court.  The Court will follow its usual procedure by posting a Notice of Tentative Ruling and/or Questions for Hearing before the hearing date.
(Please see below for ZOOM access and information).
The Court’s procedures for ZOOM appearances are set forth below.
Although the presumption is that motions will be resolved on the papers, the parties shall notice motions on an
open and available date
on the Court’s Civil Law and Motion calendar – see available hearing dates below.
