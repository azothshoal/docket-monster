.
Civil
Motions:
Hearings on civil motions will be
conducted in person or via Zoom video conference, as determined by the
court.  Information on the hearing format will be posted on the case
docket
Case
Management Conferences:
All case management conferences will be held via Zoom video webinar unless the
court determines that an in-person proceeding would aid the court.
Settlement Planning Calls and Settlement Conferences:
Settlement planning calls will take place via Zoom.  Most
settlement conferences will take place in person in the Oakland
courthouse.  The court will determine whether a settlement conference will
be conducted in person or by Zoom video conference, and will seek the parties’
input as part of the settlement planning process. For Zoom settlement
conferences, all counsel must ensure that their participants have downloaded
the Zoom application onto a device with a working camera and
microphone.
Counsel shall do a test run with all of their
participants prior to the settlement conference to eliminate all
equipment-based problems.  Audio-only participation is
prohibited.
Unless otherwise indicated, Judge Ryu will
conduct settlement conferences in her designated non-public Zoom Meeting
“room.”   The Zoom Meeting ID is available on Judge Ryu’s webpage as
well as the ECF docket entry for the scheduling of the settlement conference.

The court will issue scheduling orders for all settlement conferences referred to Judge Ryu.  Please contact Ivy Garcia at (510) 637-3639 if you have questions that are not addressed by the scheduling order.
